03 : About me

Who I am

I'm a curious teenager and high school student from Zabrze, Poland, who is fascinated by IT technologies and the possibilities they offer. My first brush with coding happened back in primary school, thanks to my math teacher, who was convincing his students to come after classes to an IT club and take a part in C++ lessons he was giving. I accepted the invitation and, looking back, I see it was a milestone. It was then that I realised coding is logical and powerful, but more importantly- fun and extremely addictive.

What happened next

When I was already in secondary school, I took a subject called 'basics of programming'. That's where I began my journey with Python. I was initially thrilled about the classes, but I soon noticed that the pace of learning was pretty slow, and due to unequal expirience levels in my class we weren't going anywhere. I knew I had to be considerate, but I also wanted to aquire coding skills at the same time, so I took the matters in my own hands.

My self-development path

Currently, I'm developing my Python skills through an online course from ZtM, where I created along many fascinating projects and still have a couple of others in store. As my next step I plan to dive in Python in Business course

I'm thrilled about your visit, so please take a look around here and feel free to contact me in 4:Contact section.
