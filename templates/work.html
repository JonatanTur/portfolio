Pass Checker

Nowadays, it's a lot to worry about when it comes to security in the web. To make you sure that your account is secure and your password has not been leaked, I created this project.

How does it work?

it's simple. The script communicates with haveibeenpwned.com API and searches for all the occurrences of your password it their database. Now you may wonder whether sending your password through web is secure. Good question, but luckily, PassChecker uses sha1 encryption which makes your data irrelevant for potential web spies. Additionally it sends only 5 first characters of your encoded password. The check, whether the passes from database are fully the same as yours, happens on your computer
